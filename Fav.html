BUGATTI DIVO

Introduction

The Bugatti Divo is a masterpiece of engineering and artistry, a symphony of power, precision, and audacious design. Unveiled as a tribute to Albert Divo, a racing legend who brought glory to the Bugatti name in the 1920s, this hypercar epitomises exclusivity and exhilaration. With only 40 units ever produced, the Divo is a crown jewel in Bugatti’s pantheon, meticulously tailored for those who demand unparalleled performance and luxury.

Specification

Performance

  • Engine: 8.0-litre quad-turbocharged W16
  • Power Output: 1,500 PS (1,103 kW)
  • Torque: 1,600 Nm (1,180 lb-ft)
  • Top Speed: Electronically limited to 380 km/h (236 mph)
  • Acceleration (0–100 km/h): ~2.4 seconds
  • Lateral Acceleration: 1.6 G
  • Weight Reduction: 35 kg (77 lbs) compared to the Bugatti Chiron
  • Downforce: 456 kg at top speed, 90 kg more than the Chiron

Transmission

  • Gearbox: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)

Chassis & Aerodynamics

  • Chassis: Lightweight monocoque structure made of carbon fibre
  • Suspension: Adaptive, optimised for cornering dynamics

Aerodynamics

  • Enlarged front air intakes for better cooling
  • Custom front splitter and rear diffuser for improved airflow
  • Fixed rear wing generating additional downforce

Dimensions

  • Length: 4,610 mm
  • Width: 2,038 mm
  • Height: 1,212 mm
  • Wheelbase: 2,711 mm
  • Weight: 1,961 kg (4,324 lbs)

Brakes

  • Front Brakes: 420 mm
  • Rear Brakes: 400 mm

Tyres

  • Front: 285/30 R20
  • Rear: 355/25 R21

Interior & Features

  • Materials: Alcantara, carbon fibre, and titanium with Divo-specific colour accents
  • Infotainment: Minimalist setup tailored for a focused driving experience
  • Seating: Sport seats with enhanced lateral support
  • Customisation: Extensive options for bespoke personalisation

Production

  • Units Produced: 40 (all sold before public reveal)
  • Price: Starting at €5 million (£4.3 million approx.)
