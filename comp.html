Top 3 Super Fast Cars Comparison

A comparison of the most powerful and fastest hypercars: Bugatti, Koenigsegg, and Ferrari

Bugatti Chiron

A supercar that defines speed, luxury, and innovation. The Bugatti Chiron is engineered for ultimate performance on the road.

Specifications

  • Power: 1,479 hp
  • Torque: 1,600 Nm
  • Top Speed: 261 mph
  • 0-60 mph: 2.4 sec
  • Price: $3 million

Koenigsegg Jesko

A masterpiece of Swedish engineering, the Koenigsegg Jesko is designed for ultimate performance on both road and track.

Specifications

  • Power: 1,499 hp
  • Torque: 1,550 Nm
  • Top Speed: 251 mph
  • 0-60 mph: 2.5 sec
  • Price: $5 million

Ferrari SF90

The Ferrari SF90 is a hybrid hypercar that blends incredible performance with cutting-edge technology.

Specifications

  • Power: 986 hp (combined)
  • Torque: 800 Nm
  • Top Speed: 211 mph
  • 0-60 mph: 2.5 sec
  • Price: $625,000
